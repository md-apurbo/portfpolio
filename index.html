My Professional Story

Hello! I'm a passionate and detail-oriented Ethical Hacker dedicated to strengthening digital defenses. With a deep interest in offensive security, I specialize in proactively identifying security flaws in web applications, networks, and systems.

My methodology is built on a "think like an attacker" mindset to provide a "defend like an expert" solution. I am committed to a culture of continuous learning to stay ahead of emerging threats and am driven by the challenge of solving complex security puzzles.

My goal is to help organizations protect their valuable data and maintain a robust security posture.
